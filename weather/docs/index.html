Site Description

This site was made with the intentions of making it easy for people to look at the current, local weather conditions.

Site Name

The name of the site is "rainraingoaway.org" as a playful way of telling them that the site is about the weather by referring to a childhood song. People tyically refer to this website as RRGA.

Purpose Statement

The purpose of this site is to provide weather conditions at any given time. Someone visiting this site will easily be able to see the forecast, the current weather conditions, and the high's and low's of the day.
