Target Audience

The target audience of rainraingoaway.org web site is described below:

  • Who: People who are looking to know what the current weather conditions are for travel or other purposes.

  • Age: 15 - 100

  • Income: Any

  • Motivation: Provide information to people wanting to know the most up to date weather conditions and forecasts.
